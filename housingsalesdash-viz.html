Housing Sales Dashboard Tableau

Introduction:

I built an interactive Tableau dashboard to explore housing sales in King County, Washington, focusing on how price and demand vary by time, location, and property characteristics. The report combines calendar and slider filters with line charts, distributions, a regional map, and condition vs. view heatmaps so users can drill into specific months, neighborhoods, and home features (like square footage, bedrooms, and bathrooms) to understand patterns in pricing and inventory.

Conclusions:

This Tableau project is an exploratory data analysis of King County housing sales, using interactive filters, maps, and distributions to uncover how prices vary over time, across neighborhoods, and by property features.

  • Heatmap and filters show which calendar days, build years, and square foot ranges are most associated with higher home prices.
  • The map clearly identifies higher priced clusters around key areas of Seattle, revealing geographic patterns in housing value.
  • Cleaning and preparing housing transaction data, then structuring it for analysis in Tableau (dates, price bins, bedroom/bathroom counts, condition and view categories)
  • Building interactive filters and parameters to let users slice the data by time and property characteristics
